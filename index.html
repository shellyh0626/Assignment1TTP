Adopt. Donate.

Every donation, no matter the size, has a profound impact on the lives of the animals we serve. Your kindness and generosity inspire us to work harder and advocate for their well-being. Together, we are making a positive change in their lives. Thank You!

Donate Items
Secondhand Items: We will happily accept your second-hand pet items. We ask that any used items are cleaned prior to donating. We can accept:
  • Unopened and unexpired food and treats
  • Blankets, Towels, Sheets
  • Leashes and Harnesses
  • Crates and Carriers
  • Enrichment Toys
  • Unopened Litter, Pellets, and Hay
Please note we cannot accept open/expired food and treats, medications, pillows or mattresses. Donate Via Mail
Send Contribution: Anima Shelters of NYC at P.O. Box 10903 New York, NY 22310 - 2860
